About

Deep Industry Experience. Executive Leadership. Academic Rigor.

Venndigm was founded by Oommen Thomas, PhD — a financial economist, engineer, and educator who came to academia after a 25-year career in consulting and industry. His journey took him from engineering into finance via graduate school, and from industry into academia — a path that gave him a rare, multi-lens view of what actually drives performance inside a business.

Along the way, Dr. Thomas founded and led the IP Consulting practice at KPMG, held consulting roles at firms including Deloitte and Law & Economics Consulting Group, and served as Finance CVP at the global engineering firm AECOM. He currently holds a joint Finance and Supply Chain/Information Management faculty appointment at the D'Amore-McKim School of Business at Northeastern University's San Francisco Bay Area campus.

That combination of academic grounding and hands-on operating experience is what he now brings to mid-market companies through Venndigm Consulting — and what led him to launch The Financial Library and Project SkillStrong, initiatives that reflect a broader commitment to putting that experience to good use.

Venndigm Consulting

Academic Rigor. Deep Industry Experience. No Overhead.

Alongside my work as a business school professor, I provide independent advisory consulting to mid-market companies, focused on two questions: how to improve profitability — understanding what's actually driving margin — and how to prepare a business to maximize value at exit.

Mid-market companies often face a frustrating trade-off: big consulting firm prices for advisory quality, or less expensive help of uncertain depth. Working independently and lean means clients get the benefit of academic rigor and decades of hands-on industry leadership, without funding a firm's overhead or a rotating cast of junior staff.

If your margins have drifted and you're not sure why, or you're considering a potential exit, I'd welcome a conversation.
